About JAGARAN

The "Jagaran Cultural Festival" is a historic initiative by Tamaddun Shilpigosthi. It is not just an event, but a movement to revive value-based culture and provide a refreshing escape from the busyness of daily life.

Our Mission

To counter vulgarity and cultural degradation by promoting an educational and enlightening culture. We believe culture reflects the stories of joy, sorrow, and emotions, and has the power to spark a revolution in society's mindset.

Our Vision

To build a healthy society through the practice of wholesome culture. We aim to bring together families, friends, and relatives under one roof to celebrate art, values, and humanity.

What to Expect

Cultural Extravaganza

Drama, Short-film screenings, Musical performances, Poetry, Mimicry, Mono acts, and Children's group presentations.

Special Guest

An exclusive evening with the nationally acclaimed singer, Dr. Haidar Saif, known for his cultural depth.

Exhibitions & Art

Curated exhibitions on "Values of Life" and "Social Issues", along with captivating Calligraphy showcases.

Competitions

Exciting contests in Arabic Calligraphy, Photography (Theme: Humanity), and Reels Making.

Kids' Zone

A dedicated immersive Playground and Game Zone for children to enjoy.

Open Stalls

Spaces for stalls showcasing products, crafts, and interactive activities for visitors.
